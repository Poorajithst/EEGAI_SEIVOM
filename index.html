About Us (E will be the solution)

In this Global Pandemic, Covid is mercilessly affecting many people’s life. Many field workers, Low wage workers and daily wage workers are struggling due to lockdown, without work! without income! without food! and what not!

So, a group of Like minded people have decided to step out of our comfort zone, to make a small change if not big, for our own society in the longer run. All the heart felt messages and immense support we received, have constantly kept us highly motivated. We have strived harder than yesterday, to give a better tomorrow for our future generations.

Here we have all gathered together for a dream which will not be far from reach, if multiple hands join together hoping to make a progress towards a better world. Thus, we have successfully taken our first step in turning our community’s dream into a reality.

Our Mission

"Lending our hand to the people in need and lifting them up to a better place" Even the smallest of the gesture from each one of us could be the kindest of gesture to them and would eventually change their little world ! Thus, We have set out seeking to spread smile and joy all across our society by lending our hands. In addition to assisting people with their materialistic needs, we are also planning to go one step further by equipping the children with knowledge and guide them for a better future. This will be a continuous effort from our side where we would constantly keep opening doors of opportunities to them. We majorly thrive to kindle them for explore new horizons.

Our Vision

Through this, we are looking forward to break the existing barriers of knowledge sharing and create awareness amongst them.Right from helping someone with food to addressing various sensitive issues that is affecting our society through awareness programs, we are planning to focus on all fields upfront, thriving to make this society a better place . Our future plans include conducting various workshops and the money raised through those events and workshops would be spent for a noble cause. *Let the change begin with us*.
